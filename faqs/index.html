Frequently Asked Questions

If any of your questions are not addressed, feel free to send us a message.

Do Trillium tabletops fit over my existing table?

Yes, they are designed to adhere to any textured surface.

Do I install it onto the table myself?

Yes, it is very simple. You will need a pencil, a pair of scissors, and a hair dryer. It will take about fifteen minutes.

Does any picture work?

Yes, with the exception of copyright protected photos. High resolution digital is preferred, but we can even use old film photos if that's what you want on your table.

Can you make changes to the photo?

Yes, we are professional photographers with extensive retouching experience. You will be surprised what we can do.

Are there additional charges for retouching the photos?

Basic "touch up" is included. Any retouching beyond basic enhancements will be charged on a case-by-case basis. Quotes will be made before you make a commitment.
