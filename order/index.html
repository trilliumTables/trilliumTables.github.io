Before you order

Make sure that you have accurate measurements for your table as specified in our measurements page before placing your order.

When you are ready, submit your image and size in the form below or via email. We will build a mock-up and send it back to you for approval. Once approved, you will be sent a link to pay for the order.

Note: If you would like to send more than one image, submit each image separately using the form below, or send them via email.
