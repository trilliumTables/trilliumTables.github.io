Measuring Your Table

These tabletops go right over what you already have. Accurate measurements are very important.

How to Measure

Measure the exact length of the flat part of the top of your table, then measure the exact width. If your table has a rim that stands above the table top, measure inside of the rim. That's all there is to it.
